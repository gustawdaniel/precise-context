# Product: Precise Context

**Precise Context** is an advanced cold outreach tool designed to extract meaningful data about leads from various conversation sources, such as WhatsApp and LinkedIn.

## Core Value Proposition
*   **Data Extraction**: Automatically parses conversation history to identify potential leads.
*   **Intelligent Scoring**: Analyzes the depth and relevance of conversations to score potential leads.
*   ** Engagement Focus**: Helps sales teams contact only the most interesting leads in a way that is personalized and engaging.
*   **Contextual Summaries**: Provides AI-generated summaries of the lead's needs, pain points, and interests based on their communication history.

## Target Audience
Sales teams, business development representatives, and agency owners looking to optimize their outreach workflow and increase conversion rates by focusing on high-quality interactions.
